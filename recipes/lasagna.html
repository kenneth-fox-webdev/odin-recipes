Lasagna

Recipe Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its rich layers of pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melty mozzarella. Believed to have originated from the Emilia-Romagna region of Italy, this comforting dish has become a beloved staple in kitchens around the world. Traditional lasagna combines sheets of pasta with a hearty Bolognese sauce, typically made with ground beef or pork simmered in tomatoes, garlic, onions, and Italian herbs. The layers are nestled between smooth cheese fillings, topped with grated Parmesan, and baked until bubbling and golden brown. The balance of textures and flavors—from the slightly crisp top layer to the tender, saucy center—makes lasagna both satisfying and indulgent.

While many variations exist, the essential spirit of lasagna lies in its slow-cooked richness and generous layering. It’s a dish often reserved for special occasions or family gatherings, not only because of its hearty nature but also due to the love and patience required in its preparation. Each layer builds upon the next, blending flavors and creating a cohesive and deeply flavorful meal. Whether served with a side of garlic bread or a simple salad, lasagna is a meal that brings people together and leaves a lasting impression of warmth and home-cooked comfort.

Lasagna Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 2–3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 500g (1 lb) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 800g (28 oz) crushed tomatoes (canned or fresh)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup red wine (optional, but adds depth)
  • Fresh parsley or basil, chopped (optional garnish)

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 450g (1 lb) ricotta cheese (or cottage cheese)
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Other Ingredients:

  • 9–12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Additional Parmesan for topping
